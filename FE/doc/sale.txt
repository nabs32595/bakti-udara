6.0	PROCEDURE DETAILS

6.1	Enquiry (RFQ) - Spares
6.1.1	Operation shall monitor and receive RFQ from interested parties (OEM Customer) thru email or system.
6.1.2	Operation shall review RFQ to clear information in the RFQ
6.1.3	If found any discrepancies in RFQ,  Operation shall inform to the relevant to interested parties for the amendment.
6.1.4	If no discrepancies found, Operation shall email the RFQ to OEM.
6.1.5	Operation shall review the OEM quotation to ensure information are tally with the RFQ.
6.1.6	If found any discrepancies in RFQ,  Operation shall inform to the relevant to OEM for the amendment 
6.1.7	If no discrepancies found, Operation shall email or upload the quotation into the interested parties system.

6.2	Order Processing
6.2.1	Operation shall receive notification on PO readiness from the relevant interested parties via email.
6.2.2	Operation shall collect and review the PO.
6.2.3	If any discrepancies on PO, Operation shall inform the interested party for amendment
6.2.4	If no discrepancies on PO, Operation shall email the PO to OEM
6.2.5	Operation shall receive order confirmation from OEM. 
6.2.6	Then, Operation shall forward the order confirmation to the relevant interested parties
6.2.7	Operation shall monitor the spares delivery based on the delivery terms stated on the order confirmation .
6.2.8	Operation shall notify interested party on the readiness of consignment for pick up via email
6.2.9	Operation then shall receive notification of delivery from OEM via email
6.2.10	Operation shall proceed for invoicing.
